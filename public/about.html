About Us

TeenHelper connects motivated teens with local opportunities. From coaching and tutoring to odd jobs and pet care. Our mission is simple: help teens earn, grow, and give back to their communities.

It all started with a personal experience. When Sam Skuratovsky and his friends were looking for summer work training kids in sports, they realized they weren’t alone. Plenty of teens were eager to earn money and gain experience. What they needed was a way to connect with people in their neighborhoods.

That’s how TeenHelper was born.

Meet the Team

Sam Skuratovsky

Founder, TeenHelper

Sam is a student at Greenwich High School and a proud member of the school's basketball team. In between classes and practice, Sam spends his time learning computer programming, something he’s been exploring with his dad since middle school.

He founded TeenHelper to solve a problem he and his friends were facing — finding flexible, local work as teenagers. Today, he’s driven to help others do the same.

Ilya Skuratovsky

CTO, TeenHelper

Ilya is a seasoned software engineer with over 20 years of experience building enterprise-level applications for companies large and small. When Sam came to him with the idea for TeenHelper, Ilya brought it to life — architecting the platform and guiding its technical growth.

Beyond coding, Ilya is passionate about mentoring the next generation of developers, including Sam.
